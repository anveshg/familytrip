Trip Overview - Nanjangud and Coorg

May 29th - Jun 1st 2014

Hello all family members!

This is a small information website for our trip. This site is accessible to all (mobile friendly) and has all the data reconstructed from telephonic conversations and crude mails. Please let me know if you feel any information has to be added.


The main requirements of our trip were:

  • Good, cool, comfortable well maintained transport with well behaving good attitude driver for covering 750Km to 1000Km in three to four days.
  • We should always travel in new routes to cover more places, as far as possible.
  • Neat and clean accomodation.
  • Healthy food.

Please scroll down for a detailed itinerary. We hope all our requirements will be fulfilled and that our trip will be a happy and a memorable one!
