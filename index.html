Trip Overview - Nanjangud and Coorg

May 29th to Jun 1st

 

Hello all family members, the main requirements of our trip were:

  • Good, cool, comfortable well maintained transport with well behaving good attitude driver for covering 750Km to 1000Km in three to four days.
  • We should always travel in the new route only in our happy & joyful vacation to cover more places, as far as possible.
  • Neat and clean accomodation.
  • Healthy food.

We hope all our requirements will be fulfilled and that our trip will be a happy and a memorable one!
