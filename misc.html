Food and Supplies

Note: We have an 18 seater AC bus which will provide a room-cooled storage solution as well.


Kits

We will need to carry certain food items and these may be packed and given as kits to each person. A kit may include:

  • A fruit
  • Dry Fruits
  • Chips Packet
  • Biscuits
  • Water Bottle

Essentials

Apart from this, we will also need to carry 20L water cans with us on the trip (atleast three) and also a waste water can for usage. One of the cans can be kept with us in the coach which will be cooled by the AC.

We may need to find a pump for drawing water from these cans

Other Supplies

  • 2L Soft Drink Cans
  • Tissue Papers
  • Paper Plates and Cutlery
  • Old Newspapers
