Kutta
Kutta, a small town in Kodagu, is the entry point to the Nagarhole National Park, especially for those coming from Kerala side. Location wise Kutta is a few kilometers from the Kerala-Karnataka border and the road that connects Kerala and Karnakaka pass via Kutta.

There are a plenty of homestays in Kutta. Two of them (Brahmagiri or Kaveri) have been selected and the inlusions are dinner and breakfast. (Non-veg at an additional cost and dishes can be chosen prior to arrival)
Kaveri homestay will be preferred as it is a smaller place and we will have the whole place to ourselves on booking.

Napoklu
Napoklu is a major base camp for several trekking spots like Thadiyandmol, Nishane-motte etc...

At Napoklu, accomodation at a tented homestay has been arranged. Two rooms are provided for changing and storage. The place is a small homestay and we'll be the only people there. On the whole, there are three bathrooms available. The inclusions are dinner, breakfast and lunch for the next day.
The tents are standard 3 men dome camping tents and in case of rains, emergency provisions are available for sleep inside in the house and the 2 rooms. The indoor provisions are ready for this.

Transport Details

Note: The vehicle will be with us throughout the trip.


The transport option that we are considering is an 18 seater AC coach. The charges include the driver's BA/TA ( 500 per day ). They do not include toll and parking at actuals

The coach has been charged for 1000 Kms distance accounting for 250 Km per day. The per Km charge is approximately 27 per Km

Another option that we have for transportation is an 18 seater Non-AC coach. The per Km charge is approximately 21 per Km

A point to note here is that as per our crude distance calculation, we will be covering only 732 Kms out of the charged 1000km.
